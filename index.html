About Me

My academic and professional journey across three continents – Indonesia, the United Kingdom, and the United States – has been a rich tapestry of cultural immersion and learning. This unique international exposure has not only broadened my horizons but has also ingrained in me a deep cultural consciousness and adaptability.

My unwavering passion lies in the food industry. I firmly believe that food is more than sustenance; it's a vibrant medium for cultural expression and connection. This belief fuels my ambition to establish a global food enterprise, specializing in Indonesian cuisine. My vision is not just to share a taste of Indonesia with the world but to do so sustainably - with a steadfast commitment to environmental, social, and economic sustainability. This venture, I believe, will not only celebrate Indonesian culinary art but also contribute to a more interconnected and responsible global food community.
